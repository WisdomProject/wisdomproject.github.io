Relationship to Self

3 December, 2024

Calm, presence, self-connection, and mastery over one's emotions are essential components of a good life. Babies are not born with the anxiety, emotional detachment, addictive behaviors, depression, or skittishness that can plague us in later years—and, that which has been conditioned into us can be healed from. You will leave our work together equipped with tools to emotionally understand and regulate yourself with skill, and—leveraging adult neuroplasticity—real, lasting breakthroughs in how you feel day-to-day.

Key outcomes in this focus area include:

  • Transforming depression, intrusive thinking, and sadness;
  • Eliminating inner guilt and shame;
  • Breaking through self-sabotage and procrastination;
  • Developing skills in managing and overcoming anxiety;
  • Developing presence, attunement, and a healthy relationship to the moment-at-hand;
  • Learning to regulate your emotions, and master your internal state;
  • Developing healthy self-love and self-compassion;
  • Learning to trust yourself, and your own intuitions;
  • Building a robust and reflective personal identity and value-set;
  • Coming to understand your unique needs and self-coordinates, in love, work, and life;
  • Developing healthy habits for self-connection, personal growth, and upkeep of mental-physical-emotional health.
